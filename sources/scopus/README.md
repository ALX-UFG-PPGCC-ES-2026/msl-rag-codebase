# Scopus

- Acesse https://www.scopus.com/, logue e selecione "Advanced Search".

- Cole a string no campo da query:
```
TITLE-ABS-KEY("source code" OR "source codes" OR "codebase" OR "code repository" OR "code search")
AND TITLE-ABS-KEY("Retrieval-Augmented Generation" OR "Retrieval Augmented Generation" OR "RAG")
AND TITLE-ABS-KEY("LLM" OR "Large Language Model" OR "GenAI" OR "Generative AI")
```

- Exporte os resultados em formato BibTeX (.bib).
